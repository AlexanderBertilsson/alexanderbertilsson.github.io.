A New Era of Competition

We are building a comprehensive platform to elevate the entire 40K experience. Our goal is to provide players and coaches with everything they need to succeed. Our platform will allow you to create your own team and manage it, create individual profiles, and track your stats across all our tournaments and events.

We will host a continuous series of high-quality tournaments and events, culminating in thrilling playoffs to crown the champions. To truly create a new era of competition, we will aim to provide live coverage and real-time updates that can be followed by audiences directly in the app. Imagine seeing pairings and scores updated instantly, bringing the thrill of the tabletop to spectators everywhere.
